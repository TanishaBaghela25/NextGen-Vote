FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Is my face data stored securely?
Yes. Face data is stored as numerical vectors (not raw images) in encrypted pickle files, bound to your Aadhaar number. No one can reverse-engineer your face from the stored data.
Can I vote more than once?
No. The system enforces a strict one-vote-per-Aadhaar policy at the database level. Even if someone tries to bypass the UI, the database constraint prevents duplicate votes.
What if face verification fails?
The system retries up to 5 times automatically. Ensure good lighting, remove glasses if needed, and face the camera directly. If issues persist, contact our helpline.
What documents do I need to register?
You need your 12-digit Aadhaar number, EPIC/Voter ID number, and a registered mobile number. Email is optional but recommended for OTP backup.
Is my vote anonymous?
Yes. While we record that you voted (to prevent duplicates), the specific party you voted for is stored separately and cannot be linked back to you in public reports.
